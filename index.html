CATCH A POKE!

How to Play:

  1. You'll get a set of three different Pokemons
  2. The goal is to catch as many unique Pokemons as possible
  3. To catch a pokemon, click on the image of the Pokemon.
  4. You have 10 tries per round after which you get the results of the round and the game refreshes.
